Know more about the Student Activity Center


The heart of the University's academic ceremonies and social and official functions are held in the SAC. This conventionally designed but fully-lighted and fully-air-conditioned activity center renders a dramatic transition between the contemporary CME, COE, and COED buildings to the accustomed CTU Danao configuration, and the linkage between the academic hub and the vibrant campus sports and cultural core.

The student activity center features a cafe-style foyer for collaboration and socialization, connecting to a newly-established outdoor open space as dance and theater rehearsals and flexible meeting bays. Purposely built to host multiple campus programs, this 400-sqm facility also provides a new venue for university wide gatherings. This center offers an indoor facility's convenience, wherein other stakeholders' need for space for celebrations and occasions is accommodated. This facility is a new step in bringing the academic, cultural, athletic departments, and the community closer together.
